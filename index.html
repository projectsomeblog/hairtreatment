How Capillary Treatments Work

The process of capillary treatment involves a combination of in-clinic procedures and a personalized home care routine. A typical treatment plan may include the following steps:

  • Scalp Cleansing and Preparation: Initial sessions focus on cleaning the scalp, removing buildup, and creating an optimal environment for treatment.
  • Application of Strengthening Actives: Specialized formulations are applied to nourish hair follicles and enhance hair strength.
  • Adjunct Therapies: In some cases, therapies such as low-level laser treatment may be used to improve circulation and stimulate hair growth.
  • Personalized Home Care: Patients receive tailored instructions on hair care routines and the use of topical products at home to maximize and sustain treatment results.

This combination of clinical care and at-home support ensures long-lasting results rather than temporary improvements. Patients are guided through every step to ensure safe and effective treatment.

Clinical Evaluation: The Foundation of Effective Care

Before beginning treatment, every patient undergoes a thorough evaluation to identify the underlying causes of hair loss or scalp problems. This assessment allows the clinician to determine whether the patient requires only capillary treatment or a combination of clinical therapy and surgical intervention, such as a hair transplant using FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) techniques.

Even when surgical procedures are indicated, capillary treatments play a critical role in preparing the scalp for surgery and supporting hair health after the procedure, ensuring long-term results and minimizing complications.

Supporting Hair Health Beyond the Clinic

Modern capillary care also emphasizes lifestyle and at-home practices that promote healthy hair. Nutrition, stress management, and proper hair care routines are recommended alongside professional treatments.

Mesotherapy involves administering nutrients and active compounds directly into the scalp to stimulate hair follicles and improve local circulation. This technique enhances the overall effectiveness of the treatment plan and is a popular option in Brazil for patients seeking accelerated hair growth and strengthened hair fibers.
